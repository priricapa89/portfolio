With Prof. Erik Brynjolfsson — MIT House, Davos 2025
Prof. Brynjolfsson leads the Stanford Digital Economy Lab and is one of the most rigorous economists studying how AI reshapes labor markets. We discussed his "Turing Trap" thesis — the danger of building AI that replaces humans rather than augments them. His argument: the real opportunity is in human-AI collaboration, not substitution. A framing I keep coming back to when I think about what the AI era actually means for people.

2026 · Cambridge, MA
Harvard Quantum Symposium — Quantum & AI Impact Series

A day inside Harvard's quantum research conversation — panels featuring Qblox, QuEra Computing, and IBM Quantum. The central question: are we at the quantum ENIAC moment? The researchers in the room were cautious. The investors were not. That tension is the most interesting signal to track right now.
